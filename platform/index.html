The idea

drope is where independent designers release limited drops — timed, numbered, then archived. The product had to feel like a gallery and a sneaker app at once: calm, editorial, but built for the rush of a launch.

I designed and built the full experience — the home feed, individual drop pages with live countdowns, item pages, brand profiles and the checkout flow — as a cohesive dark interface that puts the product photography first.

A finished product — design and build are complete. It hasn't launched publicly yet; a launch is planned. Source code is private; the screens below are a demo presentation.
